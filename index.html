How FundPath works
Built on established portfolio theory, explained in plain English

FundPath uses Claude AI to apply standard portfolio construction principles to your personal situation. Here is exactly what goes into every blueprint and score.

Blueprint generator
Your age, risk tolerance, timeline and goals are fed into Claude AI, which applies Modern Portfolio Theory principles to suggest a diversified allocation. All holdings are illustrative examples only, not personalised advice.
FundPath Score
A 0 to 100 score grading your portfolio across five factors. Each factor is weighted equally. The score reflects how well your allocation is positioned given current market conditions and is updated weekly using publicly available market data.
Illustrative projections
Where shown, projections use historical average returns for the asset class mix in your blueprint. They are illustrative comparisons only, not predictions. Past performance does not guarantee future results.
How the FundPath Score is calculated
Diversification (20%)
How spread your allocation is across asset classes, sectors and geographies. Higher score means lower concentration in any single area.
Rate sensitivity (20%)
How exposed your portfolio is to interest rate changes, based on your bond and fixed income allocation relative to your timeline.
Inflation shield (20%)
How well your asset mix historically holds up during inflationary periods. Equities and real assets score higher than long-duration bonds.
Recession buffer (20%)
How resilient your portfolio has historically been during economic contractions, based on your defensive vs growth allocation split.
Concentration risk (20%)
Whether any single holding, sector or geography makes up an outsized share of your portfolio. Penalises heavy single-stock or single-sector exposure.

Common questions
What is the Weekly Market Pulse?
Every Monday, Pro and Investor+ subscribers receive a personalised email showing how that week's market events, from sources including Reuters, CNBC, MarketWatch and Yahoo Finance, relate to their specific portfolio blueprint. It is generated by AI and tailored to your holdings and allocation. It is the reason people stay subscribed month after month.
What is Beginner Mode?
Beginner Mode is a free analysis option that generates your blueprint in plain English with every investment term explained as it comes up. It explains what an ETF is, why diversification matters, what bonds actually do, and what your risk level means in practice. It is designed for people who are just starting out and do not want to feel lost in jargon.
Is this real investment advice?
No. FundPath is an educational tool. Everything generated is for informational purposes only and does not constitute investment advice. All holdings mentioned are illustrative examples only. Always consult a licensed financial advisor before making investment decisions.
Why has the price changed?
We adjusted pricing to make Pro accessible to everyone, including beginners who are just starting to think about investing. At $97/year, Pro costs less than $8.50 a month, less than a streaming subscription. We believe everyone deserves good financial education at a fair price.
Can I cancel any time?
Yes. No long-term commitments. Cancel any time. Annual subscribers receive access for the full year they have paid for.
How is the FundPath Score calculated?
The FundPath Score is a 0 to 100 number grading your portfolio across five equally-weighted factors: Diversification, Rate sensitivity, Inflation shield, Recession buffer and Concentration risk. Each factor is assessed using your allocation mix and current market conditions. A full methodology explanation is available in the "How FundPath works" section on the home page. The score is an illustrative educational metric, not a regulated financial rating.
Do you send SMS alerts?
Investor+ subscribers can optionally provide their phone number to receive a short SMS preview of their Weekly Market Pulse each Monday, with a link to the full report. This is entirely optional at sign-up.
