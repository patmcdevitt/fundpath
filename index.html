Common questions
What is the Weekly Market Pulse?
Every Monday, Pro subscribers receive a personalised update explaining how that week's major market events — Federal Reserve decisions, inflation reports, sector moves, earnings season — relate to the type of portfolio they built on FundPath. It is generated by AI and tailored to your portfolio profile. It gives you a reason to check in weekly without needing to read financial news yourself.
Is this real investment advice?
No. FundPath is an educational tool. Everything generated is for informational purposes only and does not constitute investment advice. All holdings mentioned are illustrative examples only. You should always consult a licensed financial advisor before making investment decisions.
Can I cancel any time?
Yes. There are no long-term commitments. You can cancel your subscription at any time. Annual subscribers receive access for the full year they have paid for.
Why would I come back after getting my first blueprint?
The Weekly Market Pulse is designed exactly for this. Your portfolio does not exist in a vacuum — market conditions change every week. Pro subscribers get a plain-English explanation of what those changes mean for their specific allocation, delivered every Monday. Beyond that, as your life changes — new job, different timeline, more savings — you will want to regenerate and compare blueprints. Pro lets you save and compare them over time.
What payment methods are accepted?
All major credit and debit cards via Stripe. Payments are processed securely and we never store your card details.
